// FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything dealers usually ask before selling with us.

What do you need from me to get started?
Just the VIN. From there we handle the professional inspection, listing across 9+ auction platforms, exposure, and the sale — coast to coast.
Which auction platforms do you sell on?
ACV Auctions, Manheim, ADESA, America's Auctions, Backlot Cars, CarWave, OPENLANE, OVE and IAA — all managed through a single relationship and one simple workflow.
Is every vehicle really inspected?
Yes — 100% of vehicles receive a professional inspection. Documented condition builds buyer confidence and drives stronger, more competitive bids.
Do I have to transport my vehicles?
No. We bring the auction directly to your lot — no transport hassle, no downtime, and faster turnaround on your inventory.
How much can I expect to increase my selling?
Our exposure model is built to increase your selling by up to 300% — dealers see dramatically stronger results, guaranteed.
